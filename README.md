# About Me

I am a data professional with a strong foundation in analytics and business analysis, focused on turning complex information into clear insights that support practical decisions. I enjoy working across the full analysis lifecycle, from understanding stakeholder needs and defining requirements to cleaning data, building models, and communicating outcomes through dashboards and concise reporting.

I have experience working in structured, governance focused environments and collaborating with technical and non technical stakeholders. I value accuracy, documentation, and repeatable processes, and I am comfortable adapting to changing priorities while maintaining high standards in delivery.

# Core Strengths

- Data analysis and interpretation to support decision making  
- Data quality validation, process improvement, and operational reporting  
- Requirements elicitation, documentation, and stakeholder engagement  
- Dashboarding and performance reporting with a focus on clarity and usability  
- Continuous learning mindset and disciplined, delivery focused work style
